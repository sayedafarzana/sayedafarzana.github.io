about us

It was the “Rana Plaza Disaster” that sparked us to form Constructive Bangladesh (CB) with the aim of constructing Bangladesh in a constructive way. Before the birth of CB, we had no prior experience or idea of running a volunteer organization of its kind that could help the poor people in Bangladesh by the handful amount of monthly contribution from its members around the world. Today CB continues to grow by focusing on providing financial support to many of the poor students all over the country. We made a minor attempt to be in touch with Bangladesh and now with you we will define our future goals and activities. Let us make a journey together to construct our beloved country.
